Mutation in miRNA

MicroRNAs (miRNAs) are small, non-coding RNA molecules typically containing ~22 nucleotides found in human, animals, plants and some viruses which are playing an important regulatory role in the gene translation via silencing or degradation of target mRNAs.They are involved in many important biological processes and the dysfunction of microRNA has been associated with many diseases.
